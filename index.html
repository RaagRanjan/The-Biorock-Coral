Our Mission

To protect coral reefs

'The Biorock Coral' is an organisation dedicated for coral reef and aquatic life conservation. We work along with our members and volunteers for this cause.

Our members are enthusiatic people who dedicate their contributions to this noble cause. We encourage them to take part in activities like implementation of biorock technology and beach cleaning.

We believe that each person's contribution is valuable in making a difference. Together let us create a better future.

Biorock Technology

Biorock technology is used for restoration of coral reefs. Faster corals reef formation is achieved through this process. It is a cheap and effective method to restore coral reefs and aquatic biodiversity.

The Biorock Coral uses this technology at sites where corals have been damaged to restore them. We train our volunteers to implement this technology and create their very own coral reefs.
